About Glow Co.

Glow Co. was founded in 2010, to create high-quality, all-natural candles that would bring warmth and comfort to any home. Since then, we've grown into one of the leading candle companies in the country, with a wide range of scents and styles to suit every taste.

Our candles are made from the finest natural ingredients, including soy wax and essential oils, and are hand-poured in small batches to ensure the highest level of quality. We believe that candles should be both beautiful and functional, and we take pride in creating products that are not only aesthetically pleasing, but also provide a warm and inviting atmosphere.
